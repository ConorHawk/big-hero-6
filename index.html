Story

When a criminal plot threatens the hi-tech metropolis of San Fransokyo, brilliant young robotics whiz Hiro Hamada (voice of Ryan Potter) leaps into action with his tech-savvy friends, and his robot companion Baymax (voice of Scott Adsit) in Disney Animation's adaptation of the popular Marvel Comics series.

Hiro

Hiro Hamada has the mind of a genius and the heart of a 14-year-old: his amazing battle-bots dominate the underground bot fights. But when a tragic event changes everything, Hiro turns to a robot named Baymax, and they form an unbreakable bond becoming part of a band of high-tech heroes on a very important mission.
